# Project Report – Student Retail Spending Insights Across U.S. Store Settings

## Objective
To analyse student spending behaviour across various product categories and store settings in the U.S.

## Methodology
1. Loaded the dataset into Power BI
2. Performed data cleaning and transformation using Power Query
3. Created relationships and DAX measures for total spending
4. Designed visuals and applied conditional formatting
5. Implemented Row-Level Security
6. Published and scheduled refresh

## Key Insights
- Students aged 16–22 spend the most on **Video Games** and **Outdoor Sports**
- Rural stores have lower overall sales but higher Outdoor Sports interest
- Urban students spend more on **Gadgets** and **Books**
- Indoor Sports and Video Games spending shows a positive correlation

## Conclusion
Power BI effectively visualised and provided actionable insights on student retail spending patterns.
